we wanna be your danceburger.

Our work is grounded in unabashed inquiry and fully realized physicality.

We want to carve out a space where work can be both athletic and intellectual.

We are frustrated by the prevalent disconnect between movement and meaning in contemporary work.

We are interested in pursuing both physical and intellectual research at a high level.

The end result: work in which both movement and meaning are dependent upon and required by each other.

We are fully committed to following our ideas and bizarre whims as far as they will take us.

Our goal is to never take the easy way out and to accept the challenge of making intellectually thorough, culturally relevant, and fully realized work - even if the result is failure.

We relish the struggle of trying to answer the unanswerable and dance the undanceable.
